White_snow Skin Setup:

1. Add your favourite font into your skin addon folder: /addons/'yourskinfolder'/font/ (Example /addons/skin.aeon.nox.silvo/font/)

2. Open TVGuide_"fontname".txt, copy and paste into font.xml inside /addons/'yourskinfolder'/1080i/font.xml. Restart Kodi.

3. Change the values in your settings.xml inside your userdata/script.tvguide.fullscreen folder corresponding the values found in white_snow_settings.xml
